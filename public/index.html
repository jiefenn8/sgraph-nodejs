sgraph-nodejs API

/search

 ?q=[search keyword]

 &skip=[number of results to skip](Default: 0)(OPTIONAL)

 &limit=[max number of results in a response](Default: 10)(OPTIONAL)

 &depth=[Depth of additional result's related nodes and relationship detail](Default: Not implemented yet)(OPTIONAL)
